## Welcome!

My name is Hun Jae (/Hun-JAY/, 헌재) Lee and I am a software engineer living in Cleveland, Ohio. I am originally from Seoul, Korea.

My three main life values are: give back to the community, share knowledge and collaborate with others, and benefit the humankind with my skill set.

As a software engineer, I enjoy working in autunomous & agile environment, seeing the big picture of the product's vision, and making technical decisions accordingly.

After work, you'll see me drinking a fresh cold beer and playing video games with friends, and traveling on weekends to eat gourmet food <small>(but currently staying-at-home due to the pandemic)</small>. I also enjoy taking pictures of my rescued cat.

Feel free to connect with me for exchanges and networking. I am open for side/toy project opportunities. You can see my resume [here](/resume).

This is a blog made with [Jekyll](http://jekyllrb.com/). I created the template using [Bulma.io](http://bulma.io) and hosted the domain on [Namecheap](http://namecheap.com).
