## Welcome!

My name is Hun Jae (/Hun-JAY/) Lee and I am a software engineer living in Cleveland, Ohio. I am originally from Seoul, Korea. I graduated Case Western Reserve University with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science.

At work, I am mainly involved with PHP, Python, HTML/CSS/JavaScript. Outside of that, I dabble around with Ruby on Rails or iOS app (Swift).

Feel free to connect with me for exchanges and networking! I am open for side/toy projects. You can see my resume [here]().

This is a blog made with [Jekyll](http://jekyllrb.com/). I used [Bulma](http://bulma.io/) to make the template.
